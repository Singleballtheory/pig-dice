Play Pig Dice!

How to play Pig Dice:

  1. Each player takes turn rolling a dice. (click the "roll" button!)
  2. If the player rolls anything besides a one, that number is added to their current points tally.
  3. If the player rolls a 1, they lose all of their current points and it becomes the other player's turn.
  4. At any time during their turn, a player can choose to "hold". Clicking the "hold" button will add all the current points to the player's point total and end their turn.
